About Seventeen

Seventeen is a South Korean boy group formed by Pledis Entertainment in 2015. The group consists of thirteen members divided into three sub-units, each with a different area of specialization: a ‘Hip-Hop’ unit, a ‘Vocal Unit’, and a ‘Performance Unit’. Seventeen has released two studio albums and six extended plays. Seventeen has earned a reputation as a “self-producing” idol group, with the members being actively involved in the songwriting and other aspects of the group.
